Privacy Policy for BreathingBP

Last updated: November 2025

BreathingBP (“the App”) is developed by Steven Bailey. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information. BreathingBP is a wellness and breathing-exercise application. It does not provide medical advice and is not intended for diagnosing, treating, curing, or preventing any disease.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not create or collect any health data on its own. With your explicit permission, the App may access the following data from Apple Health:

  • Blood Pressure (systolic and diastolic)
  • Heart Rate

This data is read-only and is displayed to you for wellness and relaxation purposes. The App does not write any information to Apple Health.

2. How We Use Your Information

The App uses your Apple Health data solely to:

  • Display your blood pressure and heart rate readings within the App
  • Help you track your breathing sessions and general wellness

No personal information, including HealthKit data, is ever transmitted off your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your health information stays on your device at all times.
  • No data is collected, stored on external servers, or transmitted to the developer.
  • Your Apple Health permissions can be changed at any time in the Health app.

4. HealthKit

The App uses Apple HealthKit solely for read-only access. The App:

  • Does not write any data into HealthKit
  • Does not share HealthKit data with third parties
  • Does not use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics, tracking tools, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect personal information.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. No information is knowingly collected from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
